We are a group of Ruby and Ruby on Rails developers from Lille, North of France. We discuss web technologies (ruby, gems, frameworks…), best practices (Agile methods, conventions, tests…) and everything Ruby-related. Whether you're a professional, an enlightened amateur or just a curious newcomer, you're welcome.

Events

We meet up upon workshops where we share knowledge on last Ruby news. Have a look to the last talks.

During Ruby Burgers, we meet up around a beer (or any other drink of course) and a burger. This is a simple excuse to share a moment together.

If you're a new comer to the Ruby / Ruby on Rails world, our Rails Mentors workshops and meetings will help you to start your journey in tech.

“ Help people design, develop and deploy
better web applications, together. ”
